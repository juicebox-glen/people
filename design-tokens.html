Colours

Page background

#FFF6EE · var(--page-bg)

Main canvas, nav, intro. RGB in --page-bg-rgb for morph.

Ink (default text & black surfaces)

#000 · var(--ink) · --ink-rgb

Same as page-transition loading black. Body copy, nav, underlines, hero chrome, poster underlay, iframe letterbox, footer & mobile menu base (often with bg-black.webp).

White

#fff

Text on dark UI, slide panels (default), product card type.

Studio panel

#E2C1A3 · var(--studio-panel-bg)

#studio-overlay slide background.

Skeleton block

#3a3a3a

Placeholder blocks (e.g. hero skeleton).

Footer & mobile menu use bg-black.webp over var(--ink). Borders commonly use rgba(0,0,0,.08–.22) on light UI and rgba(255,255,255,.18–.35) on dark UI.
